## What is Cashback?

Cashback (or "money back") is a system that allows you to recover a percentage of the money you spend on your purchases. It's like receiving a discount, but after the purchase.

## How Does It Work at BestDeals?

Our process is simple and transparent:

### 1. Explore Our Products

Browse our curated selection of Amazon products. Each product has been analyzed and selected by our team.

### 2. Purchase Through Our Links

When you click "View on Amazon" and make a purchase, Amazon pays us a small commission for referring you.

### 3. Claim Your Cashback

After buying, submit your order number through your user dashboard. We verify the purchase with Amazon.

### 4. Receive Your Money

Once the purchase is verified, we credit the cashback to your account. When you reach $15, you can request a withdrawal via PayPal or crypto.
